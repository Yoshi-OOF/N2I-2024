Impact of Climate Change

Climate change is having a profound effect on our oceans. Rising temperatures, acidification, and pollution are just a few of the issues that are threatening marine life and ecosystems.

Solutions

There are many ways we can help mitigate the impact of climate change on our oceans. Reducing carbon emissions, protecting marine areas, and promoting sustainable fishing practices are just a few examples.

About Us

We are a group of ocean enthusiasts dedicated to raising awareness about the impact of climate change on our oceans and promoting solutions to protect them.
